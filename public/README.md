# Product Pictures Storage

Place your product image files here in standard formats like `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp`, or `.gif`.

### Naming Convention
To link pictures with your product items automatically, name them:
`[Product ID].[extension]`

**Examples:**
- `1001.jpg`
- `1005.png`
- `2014.webp`

These files will be served at the root URL (e.g. `https://.../1001.jpg`) and will immediately update inside your catalog view.
